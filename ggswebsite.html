ABOUT US

Quality is what we promise at Georges Garden Shop. With an established store and a brand new workpshop, we are where all the green-minded go for their gardening needs.

Our story

Georges Garden Shop is more than a nursery. Step into a sanctuary filled with gardening inspiration, garden ideas, indigenous and flowering plants, plus a wide selection of on-trend flower pots, top quality bird feeders, exquisite patio furniture and classic garden decor.

To make your life easier, Georges Garden Shop has over time built up a directory of services allied to the gardening industry. Transform your indoor living space into a green oasis filled with luscious indoor plants, paired with stylish indoor pots and beautiful containers.

Our shop

Whether you are a serious gardener or an occasional hobbyist, this is where you will find new plants, home decoration, garden care information, gardening books, recommended horticulture providers and much more. Check back regularly for fresh gardening information and inspiration.

At Georges Garden Shop, we have an on-site expert advice from a qualified horticulturist for a one-hour consultation service and half-days workshops about caring for your green yards. Book a time with us to increase your garden knowledge!

GALLERY

Our flowers, plants and bulbs are shipped in daily to ensure the freshest and best are always stocked. We offer free deliveries for online orders over R1,500.

Store pick up is available during the week. We deliver live plants to Gauteng areas using our own vehicles. Non-living goods can be delivered nationally using external couriers.
